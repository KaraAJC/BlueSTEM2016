This group will tell you how 3D sketching works & how we work together to teach you about it.

3D Sketching

today

Summary

3D sketching is a method of creating 3D models for use in 3D computer graphic apps. Teachers have stated that professional and experienced developers have used 3D sketching for projects such as video games, movies, and animations. For 3D printing, SketchUp is the one of the apps that holds and teaches you how to utilize 3D sketching for an advantage of your upcoming projects or thoughts that you have a desire of making.

class

Lessons

Our Engineering teacher, Mr. Glover, taught us about Sketchup. We learned how to create shapes and push/pull them to create fun and weird 3D solids. Our team learned to download projects that everybody in the world makes for our own projects. We have also learned to use tools to create projects or ideas to have fun with Sketchup. We figured out how to transform projects from 2D to 3D then make them in reality. We had assistance on how to use math fundamentals for precise measurements on our projects, assignments, and ideas.
