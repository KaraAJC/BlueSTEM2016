STEM Field Trips

Medieval Times

Medieval Times is a family theater about ancient times. While at Medieval Times , we had a 4-course meal, we watched a jousting tournament, and watched a play about anti-bullying.

star

Museum of Science & Industry

The Museum of Science & Industry is one of the largest museums in the world that features many fun exhibits. While at the Museum of Science & Industry , we looked at many exhibits

favorite

Chicago's Skydeck/Giordano's

Chicago’s Skydeck is on the 103rd floor of the Willis Tower with clear observation boxes & views extending to four states. While at Chicago’s Skydeck we took pictures on the observation boxes. We also went to Giodano’s for pizza, the Bean, the Watertower, and Garrett’s.

Summary

Overall, the STEM Program's field trips were very exciting and educational. We've learned about Medieval Times, chivalry, jousting , and anti-bullying. At the Museum of Science and Industry we saw a vast variety of scientific exhibits. At Chicago's Skydeck , we learned about the history of Chicago.
